HOW TO PLAY (Single Player):

Click the colored cells to swap them, and after the swap, five new random cells will be filled with random colors. The goal is to achieve the highest possible score. To add to your score you can group similar colors together, click a goal cell (the rounded cells), and hit collect. Every connected cell that's the same color will be removed and subsequently added to your score, but there must be a minimum of 5 connected cells.

HOW TO PLAY (Two Player):

Click the colored cells to swap them, and after the swap, two new random cells will be filled with random colors. The AI will then make its own swap, spawning two more cells. The goal is to achieve the highest possible score. To add to your score you can group similar colors together, click your goal cell (the green rounded cells), and hit collect. Every connected cell that's the same color will be removed and subsequently added to your score, but there must be a minimum of 5 connected cells. The AI is also trying to collect or sabotage your cells, so choose wisely as every time the AI scores it will decrease your points. Every cell is open for moving except for the AI's goal cells (the red rounded cells), although your goal cells are also protected against the AI.

Different colored cells have different scores when collected.
