HOW TO PLAY: Click the colored cells to swap them, and after the swap, five new random cells will be filled with random colors. The goal is to achieve the highest possible score. To add to your score you can group similar colors together, click a goal cell (the rounded cells), and hit collect. Every connected cell that's the same color will be removed and subsequently added to your score, but there must be a minimum of 5 connected cells.

Different colored cells have different scores when collected.
